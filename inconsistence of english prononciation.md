# Examples 

## Words that are exceptions
 - solder : `\SAH-der\` `[sˈɒdə]` (l is silent) (like soldering some metals)
 - antennae : `\an-TEN-ee\` `[]` (the ae is said ee) (its plural, antenna, is regular)
 - hyperbole : `\hye-PER-buh-lee\` (long ee at the end)
