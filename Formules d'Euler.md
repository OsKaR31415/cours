## cosinus
$\cos(x) = \dfrac{e^{ix}+e^{-ix}}{2}$

![[formule_euler_cos.excalidraw|1200]]


## sinus
$\sin(x) = \dfrac{e^{ix} - e^{-ix}}{2i}$

![[formule_euler_sin.excalidraw|1050]]


## exponentielle

$e^{i\theta} = \cos(\theta) + i\sin(\theta)$
